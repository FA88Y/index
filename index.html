✦ New Initiative

Introducing
INDEX Junior

Empowering the next generation of innovators. INDEX Junior extends the ecosystem to school students — providing age-appropriate real-world challenges, mentorship from university students and researchers, and structured pathways to prototype real solutions.

Students gain access to simplified industry challenges, hands-on project guides, resource request systems, and a network of mentors — building genuine problem-solving skills before they even begin their degrees.
